About

From the Hill to the pulpit.

Harvard, B.A. Wisconsin Law, J.D. Andover Newton, M.Div. (honors) Ordained 2019

Patrick hails from Bartlett, Tennessee — a place that gave him a love of barbecue, SEC football, and vibrant worship. He earned a B.A. from Harvard and a J.D. from the University of Wisconsin, then spent seven years as a Legislative Assistant on Capitol Hill and nearly six years practicing law in Columbus, Ohio.

When he sensed God calling him to do more with his gifts, he answered. He earned his M.Div. with honors from Andover Newton, was ordained at Richmond's First Baptist Church, and served five years as Pastor of First Baptist Church–Church of the Brethren in Cedar Rapids, Iowa — a tenure that began amid a pandemic and a derecho that tore a hole in the sanctuary roof.

His passions: the Bible, music, mystery novels, travel, and helping people find the humor in life.
